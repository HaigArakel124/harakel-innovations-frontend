Common questions
How quickly can my client be up and running?+
Most clients are fully set up within 24–48 hours of signing up. We handle the onboarding — you just send us the business details.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?+
No contracts. All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime from your dashboard. No questions asked.
What happens if my client exceeds the lead limit?+
We'll reach out before any service interruption. You can upgrade their plan at any time or contact us to discuss overage pricing.
Can I white-label this for my agency?+
Yes — white-labelling is available on the Enterprise plan. Your clients see your brand, not ours. Contact us to discuss options.
How does the AI chatbot get installed on a client's website?+
One line of code. After onboarding, you get a single <script> tag to paste before the </body> of any website. Works on Wix, WordPress, Squarespace, Shopify — anywhere.
